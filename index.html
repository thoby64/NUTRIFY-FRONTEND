Nutrition Matters

Understanding the fundamentals of healthy nutrition

Macronutrients

Carbohydrates, proteins, and fats are the three macronutrients essential for energy production, cell growth, and body functions. A balanced diet includes appropriate proportions of all three.

  • Proteins: Build and repair tissues
  • Carbohydrates: Provide energy
  • Fats: Support hormone production

Micronutrients

Vitamins and minerals are vital micronutrients that support immune function, bone health, and numerous metabolic processes. They're essential in small quantities but crucial for overall health.

  • Vitamins: Support immunity and energy
  • Minerals: Build bones and teeth
  • Antioxidants: Protect cells

Caloric Balance

Understanding your caloric needs is fundamental to maintaining a healthy weight and achieving nutritional goals. Different activities and body compositions require different caloric intake.

  • Basal Metabolic Rate: Calories at rest
  • Activity Level: Additional calorie needs
  • Goals: Maintenance, loss, or gain

Balanced Diet

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods from all food groups in appropriate proportions. This ensures you get all necessary nutrients while maintaining overall health and wellness.

  • Fruits & Vegetables: 5+ servings daily
  • Whole Grains: At least half your grains
  • Protein: Varied sources recommended
